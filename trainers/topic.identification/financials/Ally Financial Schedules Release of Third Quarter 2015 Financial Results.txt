CHARLOTTE, N.C., Sept. 30, 2015 /PRNewswire/ -- Ally Financial Inc. (Ally) has scheduled the release of its third quarter 2015 financial results for Thursday, Oct. 29, 2015. The press release will be issued at 7:30 a.m. EDT via PR Newswire and on the Ally Media Center website ( http://media.ally.com ).
Ally will host a conference call at 9:00 a.m. EDT to review the company's performance. The call will include a review of the results, followed by a question and answer session. The call is expected to last approximately 60 minutes.
Conference Call Information: Dial 877-474-9501 (or +1-857-244-7554 for international access) at least 10 minutes prior to the start time and enter the participant code 92383492. 
The conference call will also be webcast live on Ally's Investor Relations website in the Events & Presentations section ( http://www.ally.com/about/investor/events-presentations/index.html ).
The presentation and financial supplement will be posted in the Events & Presentations section of Ally's Investor Relations website on Oct. 29, 2015, at approximately 7:30 a.m. EDT.
